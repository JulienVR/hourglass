MISSION BRIEFING

Fasten Your Seatbelts

Welcome, time-traveller. Your mission — should you choose to accept it — is to cross eras, defy timelines, and dominate the disc on every field across the space-time continuum.

This year the Hourglass Tournament leaps through the centuries. Dress as your favourite era — Roman legionary, '20s flapper, futuristic cosmonaut, medieval knight, disco diva, or anything the time-stream throws at you. The only rule? Make it iconic.

The machine is fuelled by good vibes, flat passes, and an unhealthy love of frisbee. Woodstock was last year. This year, every age of history is fair game.

⚠

Time travel may cause paradoxes. We are not responsible for any anachronistic outfits, accidental dynasty changes, or scores that defy the laws of physics.

PROTOCOL

Game Rules & Schedule

◈

Tournament Format

Divided into 2 pool phases and ranking games.

Phase 1: 3 pools of 4 teams — each plays 3 games. Top 2 from each pool advance to upper pools; remaining 6 to lower pools.

Ranking games: teams with identical results from each pool face off — all the way up to the grand final.

◎

Match Rules

  • Played 7 vs 7 on grass fields
  • Regular mixed line-ups: ABBAABBA… format (4W+3M / 4M+3W)
  • Toss decides jersey colour, field side, starting gender & O/D
  • Match lasts 50 minutes or first to 15 points
  • Half-time at 8 points or 25 minutes
  • WFDF Rules of Ultimate 2021–2024
⧗

Timeouts & Cap

  • 2 timeouts per team (not in last 5 min; max 1 min each)
  • Rounds 1 & 2: no systematic cap — ties allowed
  • Ranking games: cap +1
  • Jingle signals end — finish point then clear field
  • Winning team reports score at the game stand
♡

Spirit

At the end of the weekend, each team nominates 3 teams with the best spirit.

The Hourglass is a sporting event first — play hard, play fair, play with heart.
