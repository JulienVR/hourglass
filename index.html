Rules

◈

Tournament Format

Divided into 2 pool phases and ranking games.

Phase 1: 3 pools of 4 teams — each plays 3 games. Top 2 from each pool advance to upper pools; remaining 6 to lower pools.

Ranking games: teams with identical results from each pool face off — all the way up to the grand final.

◎

Match Rules

  • Played 7 vs 7 on grass fields
  • Regular mixed line-ups: ABBAABBA… format (4W+3M / 4M+3W)
  • Toss decides jersey color, field side, starting gender & O/D
  • Match lasts 50 minutes or first to 15 points
  • Half-time at 8 points or 25 minutes
  • WFDF Rules of Ultimate 2021–2024
⧗

Timeouts & Cap

  • 2 timeouts per team (not in last 5 min; max 1 min each)
  • Rounds 1 & 2: no systematic cap — ties allowed
  • Ranking games: cap +1
  • Jingle signals end — finish point then clear field
  • Winning team reports score at the game stand
♡

Spirit

At the end of the weekend, each team nominates 3 teams with the best spirit.

Play hard, play fair, play with heart.
